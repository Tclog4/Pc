Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before buying.

🖥️ Are the PCs tested before delivery?

Yes. Every PC goes through hardware checks, temperature testing and stability tests.

🔧 Can I upgrade my PC later?

Yes. Your build ID keeps a record of your PC, making future upgrades easier.

📱 What is build tracking?

Every PC can have a QR code that shows the build history, specifications and progress.

🛡️ How does warranty work?

Each PC includes warranty coverage. Removing warranty stickers or damaging components may affect coverage.

💷 Do you build custom PCs?

Yes. Customers can request a custom build based on budget, performance and style.

🚚 Do you ship worldwide?

Shipping availability depends on location and future expansion.
